Summary

Front-end and back-end developer specialised in Oracle technologies such as JEE (Java, Servlets, JSP, JSF, Spring, CXF...), Oracle database and PL/SQL, web services (SOAP and REST), application servers (Weblogic, Tomcat, Glassfish, JBoss) and Fusion Middleware (WebCenter Content, WebCenter Portal, Secure Enterprise Search..).

My bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering was only the first step in my never-ending learning life. I'm really interested in being up-to-date with the most demanded technologies, tools and methodologies, so that is the reason for attending so many MOOC courses.

I have been part of the whole life cycle of the applications delivered in most of the projects I have taken part: architecture design, composition of technical designs, development, unit tests, functional tests, integration tests and documentation.

During these last 10 years I have worked in several IT international companies mainly located in Spain, but also in UK and Italy, in different sectors like Telecommunications, Health Care, Defence and Food Products.
